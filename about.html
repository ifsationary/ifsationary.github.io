About Us

IFSATIONARY is a project initiated by the Language Commission of IFSA LC UGM. This project aims to assist students, especially in the field of forestry, in understanding the meaning of special terms for forestry. IFSATIONARY serves as a valuable resource for students who need clear and concise definitions to support their studies and research. Our mission is to support forestry education by offering a reliable and user-friendly terminology reference platform. Thank you for using IFSATIONARY. We hope these resources enhance your learning experience and deepen your understanding of forestry terms.
